Pecel - Indonesian Traditional Cuisine

Pecel is a typical Indonesian dish consisting of boiled vegetables drizzled with savory and spicy peanut sauce. Pecel is often found in Java and has many variations, such as Pecel Madiun and Pecel Blitar.

We present a premium pecel which we call "Pecel Marcel". What makes our pecel different from others is the quality ingredients purchased directly in the early hours of the morning at Pasar Parung, mixed by the hands of high school students, and served with a modern touch to keep up with the times and attract the interest of young people.
